UK Renewable Energy Map

This project is no longer maintained, and therefore may be unstable. A replacement map is under development. {: .notice–warning}

There are now more than 4000 renewable energy projects the UK providing 33 Gigawatt of installed capacity. The interactive map allows you to see the location of all operational sites within the UK, and find out more projects by clicking on them.

It is recommended to view this on either a laptop or desktop computer. Alternatively, you can open the map full screen using this link.
